About Me

Welcome to my channel! I am currently a Civil Engineering Ph.D. candidate at The University of Alabama, where I am advised by Dr. Kaiwen Chen. I am also pursuing ECE M.S. degree supervised by Dr. Shunqiao Sun.

My research focuses on multimodal perception, digital twins, and robotic inspection systems for the built environment, with an emphasis on language-guided control, scene reconstruction, and 3D spatial reasoning. I am particularly interested in applying these approaches to robotic inspection, physical simulation for energy analysis, and edge autonomy.
